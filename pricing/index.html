What is a conversation?

A conversation is any number of sent or received messages between a unique end-user and your product (API integration) or configured business system (helpdesk, CRM, bot, etc.) One conversation will be counted for each backend integration where messages are received. End-users, however, are free to use any number of channels (Web Messenger, SMS, Viber, etc.), and all will be counted as one conversation.. Subject to Smooch’s Fair Use Policy.

How do I reply to customers using Botgate?

Botgate is a software platform for handling conversations with end-users across any channel. You can respond to conversations either from your own product with the Smooch API or from your favorite tools and services with our built-in integrations. These includes some of the most popular customer service platforms such as Zendesk, Front, HelpScout and Slack. See all business system integrations.
