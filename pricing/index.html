Whats is an AI request?

An AI (artificial intelligence) request means a set of question & correct answer that is each meaningful question raised by the user/customer together with the relevant & correct response given by Botgate.

What does rule-based mean?

Rule-based bots dominate the user conversation and offer more yes/no and button choice responses. They don’t offer a natural conversation and they don’t understand context.

Can I make more than one chatbot? Can one work on many channels?

You can create as many as you wish. All the bots can work in every channels that Botgate support.

Will Botgate understand anything I ask?

It sure will as long as you train it properly.

Which channels are you integrated with?

Skype, Facebook Messenger, Slack, Web widget and soon Whatsapp.
